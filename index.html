What does Zoru AI actually do?+

Zoru AI automates the parts of your business that live around the appointment — bookings, scheduling, payments, reminders, follow-up and upsells — across every channel your customers use to reach you.

Do I need to be technical to set it up?+

Not at all. Most businesses are live within 60 seconds. If you get stuck, we're here to help.

Who is Zoru AI built for?+

Any service business where appointments drive revenue — salons, clinics, wellness studios, home services, automotive, dental, consultants and more.

How is this different from the booking tool I already use?+

Most booking tools stop at the calendar. Zoru handles what comes next — payments, reminders, follow-up, rebooking and customer growth. Think of it less like a diary and more like a growth engine.

Can I start small and upgrade later?+

Absolutely. Start with the essentials and add more automation, follow-up and multi-location support as your business grows.
